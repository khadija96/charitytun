About Us

If you need help during this Covid-19 crisis, we are here for you.

Let's Make Our Tunisia Better Together

Tunisia Charity is a non-profit, non-governmental organization, subject to Decree No. 88 of September 24, 2011, its tax administration M1225475, aimed at serving the community through the concerted efforts of kind people to solve the problems of their societies and help the needy people.

The association works in the field of aid to people in emergency situations, as well as in the fields of sustainable development and the fight against poverty. The association also strives to spread the spirit of cooperation and solidarity among members of society and to encourage the personal and voluntary efforts of members of civil society.
The association also creates charitable projects to care for the needy and help them rely on themselves to overcome the problems they face.
